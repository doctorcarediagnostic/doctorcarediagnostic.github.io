  • Try to follow the Diabetes Food Guide

  • Increase physical activity level, aiming for at least 30 mins per day, 5 days per week
  • Choose food high in cholesterol less often, aim for 200mg to 300mg per day
  • Choose food high in soluble fibre more often
  • Choose food high in sugar and saturated fat less often
  • Choose refined grains less often, choose whole grains more often
  • Choose food high in omega-3 more often
  • Eat balanced meals

  • Choose vegetable oil or canola oil more often,, instead of butter, ghee or lard for cooking
  • Choose food high in omega-3 more often (e.g., fatty fish, nuts)
  • Avoid food containing trans fat
